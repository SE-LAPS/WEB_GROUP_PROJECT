Diploma in BUSINESS

Make your mark on the global business landscape.

Do you have a passion for marketing, want to get into the world of finance, or are keen to learn how the economy drives business decision-making? FreeLearn programs are designed to help get you into a Bachelor of Business or Bachelor of Economics.
Our focus is to equip you with the skills you’ll need for success at university, and in your future career. The Diploma of Business can take you straight into the second year of your business or economics degree*. We also offer Foundation Studies which provides a pathway to the Diploma of Business or the first year of the Bachelor of Business or Economics.

Why choose to study Business with us?

3 reasons to start your Business studies here at FreeLearn:

  • Guaranteed path to success for your higher education.
  • Study online and physical lectures in selected courses.
  • Benefit from small classes, ongoing support and a quality curriculum produced by qualified institutes.

3 reasons to complete your studies at FreeLearn

  • Collaborated with Sri Lankan leading business schools
  • Practical experience: Benefit from internships, industry-focused capstone subjects and professional work placements while you study.
  • Central business location: Learn where business happens
