Academic English for Foundation

Successful completion of Academic English 3 allows direct entry* into Foundation Studies (with entry requirements of IELTS 5.5).

Duration

200 hours over 10 weeks per level

Fees

For course cost contact FreeLearn Sri Lanka

Entry Requirements

current education states

Outcomes

Develop the confidence to interact in social situations and learn the skills you will need to succeed in Foundation Studies or courses requiring an IELTS 5.5.

Example skills you will learn

Language and skills taught vary depending on the level you undertake but some of the skills learnt include:

  • essay writing suitable for year 1 university submission
  • academic reading and comprehension
  • presenting confidently in English
  • participating confidently in group work activities
  • time management and self directed study
  • taking lectures and reading notes
  • critical thinking and questioning
  • how to communicate with lecturers, tutors and fellow students
  • resume preparation,
  • interview preparation
  • how to have a conversation in social settings and more!

Academic English for Diploma

Successful completion of Academic English 4 allows direct entry* into our degree pathway (Diploma) programs (courses with entry requirement of IELTS 6.0).

Duration

200 hours over 10 weeks per level

Fees

For course cost contact FreeLearn Sri Lanka

Entry Requirements

current education states

Outcomes

Develop the confidence to interact in social situations and learn the skills you will need to succeed in diploma programs or courses requring an IELTS 6.0.

Example skills you will learn

  • Effective academic writing
  • Effective participation in seminars and tutorials
  • Listening and note-taking skills for academic study
  • Understanding lectures
  • Reading and researching academic texts
  • Summarising and paraphrasing
  • Listening and reading to interpret the media
  • Effective contribution to class discussions and group work

Academic English for Bachelor/Masters

Successful completion of Academic English 5 or 6 allows direct entry* into most undergraduate and postgraduate degrees (with entry requirements of IELTS 6.5 or IELTS 7).

CRICOS Code

032418F

Duration

200 hours over 10 weeks

Fees

For course cost contact FreeLearn Sri Lanka

Entry Requirements

current education states

Outcomes

Develop the confidence to interact in social situations and learn the skills you will need to succeed in Bachelor or Masters courses or courses requiring an IELTS 6.5 or 7.0 (depending on course completed).

Example skills you will learn

  • Advanced academic writing
  • Advanced participation in seminars and tutorials
  • Listening and note-taking skills for academic study
  • Understanding detailed academic lectures
  • Reading and researching complex academic texts
  • Advanced cultural interaction skills
  • Academic conversational skills and strategies


Why study UTS College Academic English?


  • Academic English is an alternative to the IELTS test
  • Academic English is recognised as meeting the English language entry requirements at a range of selected institutions in Sri Lanka (no IELTS required)
  • Academic English ensures you are prepared for study and life abroad i.e. you are prepared to succeed in International English speaking Universities.
  • has been preparing students for successful study overseas for over 30 years
