Does future focused technology ignite your interest? Do you have a passion for computers? Are you curious to discover how things work? Tomorrow's web designers, software engineers and app developers take their first step with a Information Technology degree.

Our focus is to build your skills, develop your confidence and prepare you for success at our institutes, and in your future career in Computing. We offer the Foundation Studies and a degree pathway (diploma) in Computing, which leads straight into the second year of your IT degree.
