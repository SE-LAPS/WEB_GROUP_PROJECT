Scholarships

At FreeLearn we believe in offering students, such as yourself, the ability to access education at a location that works for you. Our new offering provides the opportunity for you to start your journey with selected Sri Lankan educational institutions and in support of this, we are pleased to offer those scholarships.
Designed for students who are willing to study harder for a better future. Scholarships are 100% FREE and we hope you will take the maximum from it.

About the scholarships

Approximately $100,000 USD in scholarships including

  • 25 x full fee scholarships for a term
  • Unlimited 25% scholarships

Eligibility criteria

  • The offer is open to students who are completed o/l's or A/l's
  • Those who are not completed o/l's, must follow the foundation courses.
  • Every student will face to a common test and the top 25 students will win the scholarships.

Terms and Conditions

  • For students who completed o/l's , a/l's within 2016-2021
  • The scholarship will be applied to the student's tuition fees and shall not be offered as a cash payment nor be available as a refund
  • Offer details are subject to change
